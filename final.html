Final Flappy Bird Project

For my final project, my partner and I decided to create our own rendition of Flappy Bird. To play, simply

hit the start button on the main screen of the app! Then use any key on your keyboard to help the bird fly

and avoid the tubes. The tubes are serving as obstacles and once they are hit, the game is OVER!!

The Flappy Bird game evaluates your score by counting how long you can keep the bird flying
